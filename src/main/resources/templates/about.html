About MooDrop

MooDrop is a secure, browser-based peer-to-peer (P2P) file sharing platform designed to make file transfers fast, simple, and completely server-free.

Our mission is to provide a privacy-focused file transfer solution where users can send files directly between devices without uploading data to third-party servers. We believe your files should remain yours — private, encrypted, and under your control.

MooDrop uses modern web technologies including WebRTC for real-time peer-to-peer communication and AES-based encryption to ensure secure transfers. No accounts, no logins, and no file storage are required.

Unlike traditional cloud storage services, MooDrop does not permanently store files on any server. All transfers happen directly between connected devices, reducing risks and improving privacy.

Why We Built MooDrop

Many file sharing platforms require uploads, registrations, or impose size limits. MooDrop was created to remove these barriers and offer a lightweight, instant file-sharing experience that works across desktop and mobile browsers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make secure digital communication accessible to everyone without compromising privacy or simplicity. We continuously improve performance, security, and usability to provide a reliable experience for users worldwide.

Transparency & Trust

We value transparency. MooDrop does not collect personal data, does not track file contents, and does not store transferred information. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Contact

For support, feedback, or business inquiries, you can reach us via our official GitHub page or listed contact channels. We welcome suggestions and continuously work to improve MooDrop.
