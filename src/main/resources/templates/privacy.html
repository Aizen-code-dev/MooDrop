Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. MooDrop is designed as a secure, peer-to-peer file sharing platform that prioritizes user privacy and does not store transferred files on any server.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

MooDrop does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or passwords. We do not store, monitor, or access the files being transferred.

2. How File Transfers Work

MooDrop uses peer-to-peer (P2P) technology to connect devices directly. Files are transferred securely between connected users using encrypted channels. Once the transfer is complete, the connection automatically closes.

3. Technical & Log Data

For performance, debugging, and security purposes, limited technical information such as browser type, device type, or connection status may be temporarily processed. This data is not linked to personal identity and is not used for tracking individuals.

4. Cookies & Advertising

MooDrop may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance. If advertising services such as Google AdSense are used, third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on user visits to this or other websites.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or adjusting browser cookie preferences.

5. Data Security

We implement modern encryption standards and secure communication protocols to protect data during transfers. However, no internet transmission method is 100% secure, and users are encouraged to share files responsibly.

6. Third-Party Links

MooDrop may contain links to external websites or projects. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official communication channels.
