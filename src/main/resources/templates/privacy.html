Privacy Policy

At MooDrop, your privacy is our highest priority. MooDrop is a browser-based peer-to-peer (P2P) file sharing platform designed to allow secure, direct device-to-device file transfers without storing files on any server. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

MooDrop does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, passwords, or payment details. We do not access, monitor, store, or analyze the files transferred between devices.

2. How File Transfers Work

MooDrop uses WebRTC-based peer-to-peer communication technology. When users connect using a Remote ID, a direct encrypted channel is established between devices. Files pass directly from sender to receiver without being stored or processed by MooDrop servers. Once the transfer session ends, the connection automatically closes.

5. Data Retention

Since MooDrop does not store transferred files or personal accounts, no file data is retained after a session ends. Any temporary technical data used for performance monitoring is automatically cleared or anonymized.
